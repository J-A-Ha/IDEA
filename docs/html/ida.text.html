ida.text.information_extraction.extract_cities(words, language='all')[source]

Parameters:

  • words (list, set, or tuple) – iterable of strings to analyse.

  • language (str) – language of corpus of city names to use for comparison.

Returns:

result – extracted city names.

Return type:

list

Notes

ida.text.textanalysis.cosine_sim(input_text_list: List[str], stopwords='english') → float64[source]

Calculates the cosine similarity of two texts based on their word frequencies.

Parameters:

  • input_text_list (list) – list of strings to compare.

  • stopwords (str) – name of stopwords dataset to use.

Returns:

result – a float representing the count-vectorised cosine similarity of the inputted strings.

Return type:

numpy.float64

Notes

Takes a list of two texts. Removes stopwords based on the ‘stopwords’ argument.
